I really like this effect. Take a harmonizer effect, and rather than toggling it on and off with your foot, engage it only when you push the pedal down. The momentary switch on my HX Effects is perfect for this. With this harmonize that you can add and remove with your foot, it's like have an extra finger to hammer on and pull off, but at a crazy interval.
