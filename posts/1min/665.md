I've been out every morning in the woods doing deeper listening to music than I have in a while, and I've been pulling together new things for 'ebow choir'. It's super satisfying rendering these Late Renaissance pieces.  I caught up with harpist Monica Schley not too long ago, [who just published  some of Hildegard's piece for Harp](https://www.melbay.com/Products/31151M/songs-of-hildegard-von-bingen-for-harp.aspx). I feel fortunate just to know about this music. 

Our responsibility as artists to keep creating is very much in the air this week. This is my arrangement of Carmina Chromatico by di Lasso, from [Prophetiae Sibyllarum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prophetiae_Sibyllarum].
